About Leo/Need...

Leo/Need are a 4-piece japanese soft rock band, formed by a group of childhood friends who have learned to overcome their differences. After working hard to better themselves, they landed themselves a deal with a record company and turned professional. Led by the energetic voice of Hoshino Ichika and with the talents of drummer Mochizuki Honami, keyboardist Tenma Saki, and bassist Hinomori Shiho, they continue to go from strength to strength as a band. Their journey continues starwards!

Musically, the band covers a range of styles but primarily leans on their rock-oriented style of music, which is equal parts energetic and emotive. The band are known to bring more than just their own voices to their songs too. A number of their songs also feature famous vocaloids such as Hatsune Miku, Megurine Luka, and the Kagamine twins!

Meet the band members!

Leo/need's vocalist and guitarist. Seems aloof at first glance, but is friendly and caring when you get to know her.
She's known Saki, Honami, and Shiho since they were little, but a certain event drove a wedge between them.
Since then, she's reunited with her bandmates and has gone pro with Leo/need!
